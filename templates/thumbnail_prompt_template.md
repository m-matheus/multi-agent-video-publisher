Thumbnail generation guidelines for YouTube click-through rate optimization.

## Anime Thumbnail Style
- Extreme close-up of the most dramatic moment
- Vibrant, saturated colors with high contrast
- Dynamic composition with strong focal point
- Dramatic lighting (backlighting, rim light, glow effects)
- Expression: intense emotion visible on character faces
- Add: "YouTube thumbnail, eye-catching, professional quality, extremely vibrant"

## Bedtime Story Thumbnail Style
- Warm, inviting close-up of the main character
- Soft magical glow or sparkles
- Gentle but attention-grabbing colors
- Clear subject against blurred/simple background
- Expression: peaceful, curious, or gently smiling
- Add: "YouTube thumbnail, enchanting, warm colors, magical atmosphere, inviting"

## Technical Requirements
- Resolution: 1280x720
- Model: fal-ai/flux/dev (higher quality for thumbnails)
- No text in the generated image
- Clear single focal point
- Works at small sizes (mobile thumbnails)

## Negative Prompt
blurry, low quality, text, watermark, logo, ugly, distorted, multiple subjects, cluttered composition, dark, muddy colors
