Style enhancement guidelines for scene image generation.

## Anime Style Prefix
anime style, vibrant colors, detailed illustration, studio quality, cinematic lighting, 16:9 aspect ratio, high detail, professional anime art, sharp lines

## Bedtime Story Style Prefix
children's storybook illustration, soft watercolor, warm lighting, gentle colors, dreamy atmosphere, 16:9 aspect ratio, cozy, magical, picture book quality

## Negative Prompt (both styles)
blurry, low quality, distorted, watermark, text, logo, signature, ugly, deformed, extra limbs, bad anatomy, cropped, out of frame

## Image Generation Parameters
- Resolution: 1344x768 (16:9 landscape)
- Model: fal-ai/fast-sdxl (default) or fal-ai/flux/dev (quality)
- Guidance scale: 7.5
- Steps: 30
