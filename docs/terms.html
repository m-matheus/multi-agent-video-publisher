1. Overview

Hakase Anime Publisher ("the App") is a personal, non-commercial tool used to automate video publishing to TikTok and YouTube. It is not available to the general public and is operated solely by its author for their own content channel.

2. Use of the App

The App is used exclusively to upload videos created by the author to their own TikTok and YouTube accounts. No third-party user data is collected, stored, or shared.

3. TikTok API

The App uses the TikTok Content Posting API to upload videos on behalf of the authenticated TikTok account. Authentication tokens are stored locally on the author's machine and are never transmitted to any third party.
